About Us

Our Story

At Gelato Haven, our story begins with a deep love for gelato.

In 1997, our owner, Tan Pham, first tried gelato and fell in love. Since then, her love for gelato led to a dream.

In 2017, with no prior experience or training and just a passion for gelato, Tan started our café.

Tan made special gelato recipes from her taste and experiences.

Over time, our small café has become a special place for those who want a treat that makes them smile.
