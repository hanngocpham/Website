Welcome to Gelato Haven!

We're a cozy and charming caf√© that serves gelato, sorbet and coffee. Established in 2017 as a small business and located in the heart of Downtown Ottawa, in proximity to other popular attractions, we have quickly become a beloved destination for locals and visitors.
