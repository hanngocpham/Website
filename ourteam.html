Our Team

Welcome to Our Team page! Our team is awesome and we appreciate all the dedicated hard working and enthusiastic team members but we wanted to give a special mention to our Managers!

Brandon M.

Our Cafe Manager, Brandon, is the backbone of our daily operations. He is responsible for overseeing day-to-day activities, managing staff and maintaining inventory. He works closely with all the managers and staff to ensure the smooth flow of all operations. Brandon is an awesome manager and has been with us since 2015.

Jeremy R.

Our Training and Development Specialist, Jeremy, does an amazing job at training our new staff and keeping a positive work environment. Energetic and people-oriented, he brings enthusiasm to every task. His favourite gelato flavour is Stracciatella.

Jilwan M.

Our Inventory Manager, Jilwan, excels at efficiently managing all aspects of our inventory, including gelato and beverage ingredients, takeaway items, and more! He coordinates with vendors and ensures orders come on time. He joined the team in 2014 and has been a valued team member since.

Nick L.

Our Gelato Maker, Nick, who makes all the delicious magic happen! He is passionate about creating gelato and loves experimenting with new flavours based on the season and customer's preferences. His father was our original Gelato Maker since opening in 1997, and Nick took over in 2010.
